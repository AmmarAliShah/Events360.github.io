What We Do

1) EVENTS DESIGNING, PLANNING, COSTING & BUDGETING, EXECUTION, OUTPUT EVALUATION

  • Designing: Event designing refers to styling an event to increase its success rate. Event production and design transforms ideas into realities. For brands, event production and design creates opportunities to establish a meticulous brand identity and promote brand awareness in a controlled environment that showcases their values and brand attributes.
  • Planning: To coordinate every detail of events, from beginning to end. We ensure everything related to an event is taken care of, from idea conception to programming and day-of logistics.
  • Event Costing & Budgeting: Everything that is spent within the event budget is an investment. Given that an event's financial foundation is directly related to the event outcome, creating a thorough and realistic event budget is a key ingredient to the recipe for event success.
  • Execution: Every single successful event has a mission and a primary purpose that is accompanied by objectives and goals. Success of an event is measured by the fulfilment of these goals.
  • Output Evaluation: People who are attending any special event should enjoy themselves and should also enjoy the various and different aspects of the event. At a basic level, events are judged simply by getting the show on the road and the size of the crowd that turns up. However, at a management level, the success of events are measured in more strategic terms that are input, output and outcome. At Events360 – Design & Management we thoroughly plan the strategy for the positive output of the event as well as the brands.

2) MARKETING & SPONSORSHIP MANAGEMENT

Sponsorship is the fastest growing form of marketing in all around the world. Brands find unlimited opportunities to broaden their competitive advantage with this in mind. Despite of spending a lot of money on print advertising or on audio and television commercials, many of the well-known brands are now spending a lot less and perhaps achieving a better result by simply providing their product in the hands of potential consumers. At Events360 – Design & Management, we work & implement the event marketing plan to provide best platform to the brands to connect with their target audience by sponsoring the event.

3) BRAND MANAGEMENT THROUGH EVENTS

Effective brand management increases brand awareness measures and manages brand equity; drives initiatives that support a consistent brand message; identifies and accommodates new brand products; effectively positions the brand in the market. We at Events360 – Design & Management also plan events which are specifically focused on your brand/product.

4) PR & ADVERTISING

Sponsorship is considered a qualitative medium whereas advertising is considered a quantitative medium used primarily to reach specified business goals. Events360 – Design & Management is an exclusive partner of some of the top PR & Advertising Agencies of Pakistan to provide one-stop solution to our clients.
