What Is Sponsorship at Events360 - Design & Management?

Events360 - Design & Management – Connecting, inspiring and activating individuals around the power of events. For us, Sponsorship Is…
Connecting you to the right people in a space designed to optimize your relationship-building and deal-making. Sponsorship gives you access to the audience of our highly significant events through:

  • Face time: Meet a large number of our filtered audience at your booth, during events, roundtables, in workshops, in concerts, theatres, talent hunt programs, in fashion shows and more.
  • Fun Networking: Talk to the people you’re there to meet at our events. Host your own private breakfasts & lunches, one-on-one meetings, distribute your products and literature, arrange cocktail parties... Your prospects will get back to the office with your brand on their minds!
  • Branding: Have your brand featured on the main agenda, in emails, on our website, on all over social media, through BTL activities and throughout the event site.
  • Marketing: Showcase your products, services, solutions and offer product demos to drive demand amongst your target market.
  • Extras: Business development services, brand logo on our teasers and promos, post event marketing campaigns and much more.
